Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our integrated health and dental services.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers for both our medical laboratory and dental services. Please contact our office with your specific provider details.

How fast can I get lab results?

Most routine tests are processed within 24 hours. Critical results are prioritized and communicated immediately to your referring physician.

Can I schedule lab and dental visits on the same day?

Yes. Our care coordinators can help align your appointments across departments for maximum convenience, minimizing your time spent at the clinic.

What do I need to bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, and any relevant medical/dental records or referrals from other providers.
